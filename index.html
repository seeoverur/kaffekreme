Discover your next favorite drink and see why our loyal customers rave about our commitment to the perfect coffee experience.

Café Classics: The Foundation

Espresso

A bold, concentrated shot of rich, smooth coffee. The essential building block for many of our beloved drinks, providing a powerful and flavorful base that sets the standard for quality.

Cortado

Perfectly balanced, featuring equal parts espresso and steamed milk for a smooth, velvety texture. This drink highlights the quality of the espresso with minimal dilution.

Cappuccino

The classic foam experience: espresso with equal parts steamed milk and luxuriously thick, frothy foam. A timeless favorite for those who enjoy a lighter, airy milk component.

Flat White

Rich espresso combined with velvety microfoam for a creamy, smooth coffee. It’s slightly stronger than a latte, offering a refined taste for the true coffee enthusiast.

Latte

Our standard offering: espresso with steamed milk, lightly layered with foam. A versatile and comforting drink, ideal for adding custom syrups and flavors.

Mocha

A delightful fusion of espresso, gourmet chocolate, and steamed milk, traditionally topped with a light layer of foam. It’s the perfect blend of coffee richness and sweet indulgence.

Americano

A simple yet potent choice: espresso diluted with hot water. This preparation allows the full flavor of the espresso blend to shine through in a volume similar to drip coffee.

Drip Coffee

Our freshly brewed coffee, chosen for its clean, rich, and consistent flavor profile. A daily staple, perfect for those needing a reliable and satisfying caffeine boost.

Cold Brew

Smooth, intensely flavorful, and slow-steeped for over 12 hours. Served chilled, our Cold Brew offers lower acidity and a natural sweetness, making it incredibly refreshing.

Signature Lattes: Flavor Innovation

Our signature line showcases creative flavor combinations available with either our strong espresso or premium, vibrant matcha. These unique blends elevate the standard latte experience into a truly memorable dessert-in-a-cup.

Sweet Palm Latte

Our namesake beverage, featuring a unique, house-made syrup blended perfectly with creamy espresso or matcha. This proprietary flavor captures the essence of Sweet Palm.

Vanilla Cream Latte

A rich and creamy base of espresso or matcha topped with our signature smooth vanilla cold foam. The contrast between the warm drink and the cold topping is exquisite.

Salted Caramel Latte

Sweet caramel and sea salt layered over a shot of espresso or smooth matcha. The interplay of sweet and savory notes is highly addictive.

Southern Pecan Praline Latte

Toasty pecan and rich caramel flavors combine with espresso or matcha to deliver a decadent, regional dessert-inspired treat. A true taste of the South in a mug.

Sweet Palm Macchiato

A layered masterpiece starting with milk, our proprietary Sweet Palm syrup, and espresso poured on top, finished with a golden caramel drizzle for visual appeal and flavor complexity.

Banana Pudding Latte

This dessert-inspired latte uses a blend of banana and vanilla flavors mixed into the milk, resulting in a comforting and sweet beverage reminiscent of the classic Southern dessert.

Tea, Chai, and Refreshers

For those who prefer a non-coffee boost, our tea and refresher menu offers sophisticated and energizing options. These range from traditional, calming infusions to vibrant, plant-based energy drinks.

Chai Latte

A warming infusion of spiced black tea with cinnamon and cardamom, steamed together with milk. This aromatic blend is deeply comforting and subtly sweet.

Carolina Sweet Tea

Our nod to regional tradition: classic Southern-style iced tea, perfectly steeped and sweetened to be crisp and refreshing on any Charleston day.

Pink Palm Energy

A highly popular, plant-based energy drink featuring bright Strawberry and exotic Dragonfruit. A guilt-free way to recharge your day.
